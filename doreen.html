Doreen Marchetti

Board Director, Head of Partnerships

Doreen has over 4 decades of professional, IT experience in roles leading sales, marketing, business development and channel management for organizations ranging from Fortune 50 to mid-size to start-ups. Today, Doreen has embarked on the new startup, iSolvrisk, with the intent to collaborate and partner with relevant companies, educational institutions, and communities.

In addition, Doreen is currently a fractional VP of Sales for a software and product engineering firm, a Strategic Partnerships Advocate for an International Workforce Innovation Network and a freelance sales consultant guiding a UK based coaching and storytelling company to expand in the USA market.

Doreen is passionate about caring for her mom stricken with Parkinson's and Dementia and has embraced several groups, most notably, Confessions of a Reluctant Caregiver, in hopes to help other people manage the broken process. When not helping to grow revenue for her current muses, Doreen loves spending time cooking with her husband Michael and two grown sons and their rescue dog Tanger.
