Douglas Clemmons

General Counsel

A graduate of Fordham Law School, Douglas Clemmons founded the Clemmons Law Firm in 2014. While Douglas is well versed in many areas of law, his practice primarily focuses on financial and regulatory matters, at both the state and federal levels, and transactional law with litigation experience. His experience can best be described as an amalgamation of time and energy spent servicing the needs of individuals and entities seeking advocacy under distressed financial circumstances. This was the nature of his practice during its earliest days. Today, Douglas successfully represents clients in multiple aspects of business, from the most complex, be it litigation or entrepreneurial in nature, the end result is most often and optimal, positive conclusion.
