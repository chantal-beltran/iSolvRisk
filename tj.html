TJ Williams

Head of Marketing

In TJ's senior year of high school, he realized he had a natural ability to communicate with people of all demographics and backgrounds. He decided to put those skills to use and pursued opportunities in the field of marketing. TJ joined the iSolvRisk Inc. team while working to earn his bachelors degree in Business Administration from Livingstone College in Salisbury, North Carolina.

Currently, TJ serves as the Head of Marketing at iSolvRisk Inc., leading the team's outreach efforts and using his personal experience as both an avid golfer and basketball player to make connections and introduce iSolvRisk Inc.'s mission to the world.

Upon discovering iSolvRisk Inc., TJ was excited for the opportunity to help the company grow and put his communication skills to use; however, what motivated TJ the most was the potential he saw in the product itself. After learning about the positive impact iSolvRisk Inc. can have on so many lives, he became eager to spread the word about the company and our product.
