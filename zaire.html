Zaire Adams

Head of Design

Zaire has been an invaluable asset to our team since 2021, bringing his expertise as the Head of Design at iSolvRisk Inc. With his dynamic leadership, he spearheads our design and user experience research initiatives, igniting a passion for innovation within our organization.

Back in 2016, Zaire immersed himself in the world of Architecture at Pratt Institute during the summer, fueling his creative drive. His educational journey culminated in a Graphic Design degree from St. John's University in New York City. But Zaire's hunger for growth didn't stop there. He embarked on a self-taught exploration of various design and research methodologies, immersing himself in user experience design, product design, user interface design, qualitative research methods, quantitative research methods, data analysis, and data synthesis. These versatile skills, combined with his extensive experience, empower him to craft meaningful, data-driven, and user-friendly digital products, just like the groundbreaking one we're building at iSolvRisk Inc.

Zaire's passion for his work stems from his aspiration to reshape the way people perceive risk management. He joined our team with the exciting opportunity to transform the landscape of decision-making, relieving individuals of stress and enabling them to make better-informed choices. His dedication and enthusiasm infuse our organization with an energy that propels us towards groundbreaking achievements.
